Neural Damage (2022)

01/09/2022

Digital Painting / Collage

Society on top of the foodchain, yet nostalgic about dark past. Psychologically obvious but might seem selfish for future generations. Creation of humanoids has begun. So far good for the original species which were perspecuted for so long time. We are changing reality with point of views everynight.
