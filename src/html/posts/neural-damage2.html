Neural Damage - Vegetative Edition

01/09/2022

Digital Painting / Collage

Taggin across the midnight district can be rewarding but what about vandalism in a bit different reality. Virtual real estate is about to blow up, so as potential for expressionism in digital space. Often equipped with endless strings instead of paint buckets, these vandals are from different class of society.

Main concern however should be not the vandal itself but the law maker from another reality. What is a huge gap between regulations needed and pursued may just greaten over time. Monolitic institutions leading society to collapse, for short term good of few. Real destruction in the sight.
