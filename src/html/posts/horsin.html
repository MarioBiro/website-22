Horsin / Mad Face

2022

Digital Painting

Sometimes it's difficult to decide which layer combination can be used in final version of the artwork. I've discovered however another possibility when in similar doubts. Merge of two variations. As you are probably already guessing, that's how this product was made. Naming convention used is not following same principle tho, as you can see in details section, I've highlighted the objects which were created unintentionally and are getting my attention every time I look at the piece.
