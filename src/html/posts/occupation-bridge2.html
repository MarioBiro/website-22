An Occupation Bridge

2022

Digital Painting

Second revelation of the work is about inversion of the bad outcomes, transformation of street elements and blurry visions into something visualy interesting, calming the mind without need to deconstruct. This piece is composed of cold tones while trying to achieve positive affiliations in opposite to the original. Street elements might become clearer when observing on a different scale.
